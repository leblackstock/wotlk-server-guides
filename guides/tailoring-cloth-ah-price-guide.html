WotLK 3.3.5 Tailoring / Cloth Materials AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Sorted with Northrend, rare, and high-value tailoring materials first.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Server cooldown note: Specialty cloth cooldowns are much shorter or nonexistent here, so price cooldown cloth from material cost + profit margin, not from cooldown scarcity alone.
