WotLK 3.3.5 Drop Turn-In & Quest Page Items AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers tradeable drop-based reputation turn-ins, Darkmoon Faire drop turn-ins, and buyable quest-page items. Profession-made Darkmoon turn-ins, soulbound quest items, and normal crafting materials are excluded unless their main value here is a drop turn-in use.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Grouping rule: Similar drops are lumped together when they behave like the same market. Split them only when one item or page is clearly scarce.

    Aldor and Scryer repeatable drops.

    Repeatable Timbermaw rep drops.
  • ZG coins / Hakkari Bijous
    Zandalar Tribe turn-ins.
  • Quest pages
    Ashenvale and Green Hills missing-page markets.

Before Buying

Check tradeability first. Some old turn-in items require a quest state, reputation range, active event, commission/trinket, or specific server behavior. Soulbound, quest-only, non-tradeable, and maker-only items should not be treated as AH goods.
