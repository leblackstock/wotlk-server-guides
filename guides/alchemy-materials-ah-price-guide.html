WotLK 3.3.5 Alchemy Materials AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers Alchemy materials, herbs, Eternals, oils, transmute inputs, vendor supplies, plus a few finished prices for major flasks and potions.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Server cooldown note: Alchemy cooldowns/transmutes may be shorter or nonexistent here, so price transmutes from input cost + margin, not cooldown scarcity alone.
