WotLK 3.3.5 Inscription Materials AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers Inscription materials, inks, pigments, practical milling inputs, vendor supplies, plus a few finished prices for vellums and Darkmoon cards.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Scribe rule: Treat this as a materials-first sheet. Compare raw herbs, pigments, inks, and Darkmoon card value before milling or crafting.
