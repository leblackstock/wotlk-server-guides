Strategy notes

Best movers

  • Handful of Cobalt Bolts Wrath core
    The most reusable Wrath Engineering part. Good 5/10/20 stacks.
  • Saronite Bar / Cobalt Bar
    Use the raw-input sections to compare bar value against crafted-part value.
  • Volatile Blasting Trigger / Froststeel Tube
    Good convenience parts when levelers are active.
  • Thorium Widget / Mithril Casing
    Old catch-up bottlenecks that can sell well on thin AHs.

Do not overbuild

  • Do not craft huge 8-day walls of bombs/ammo until you know demand exists.
  • Do not list vendor-only mount parts like rare drops.
  • Convert bars/stones into parts only when the matching crafted-part section shows a clear premium.
  • For Engineering levelers, 5-stacks and 10-stacks often beat giant stacks.

Small reminders

Raw vs crafted part

Check whether bars/stones sell better raw or as Engineering parts. Convenience parts can beat raw materials, but only if the AH is thin.

Bomb/ammo demand

Bombs, sapper charges, and ammo depend on demand here. Post small first, then scale up only if they sell.

Sources / calibration

Prices are low-pop starting points, not fixed market rules. They are calibrated from public profession references, Wrath-era Engineering demand, and practical reposting behavior. Always adjust for current AH supply, guild demand, and trade-chat pricing.
