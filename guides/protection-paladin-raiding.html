WotLK 3.3.5 Raiding with your Tankadin

Prepare your consumables, select the right set, and know which Paladin tools matter on each raid encounter.

Encounter tuning

🛡️ Heavy physical damage

  • Favor armor, stamina, an armor trinket, and Indestructible Potions.

✨ Magic or mixed burst

  • Favor stamina, a resistance aura or on-use trinket, and planned external cooldowns.

🎯 Mandatory taunts or pickups

  • Use Glyph of Righteous Defense and add only enough hit for the required reliability.

⚔️ Parry-sensitive boss

  • Build extra expertise only after confirming that the boss parry-hastes on Hellscream.

🧱 Many smaller attackers

  • Block value, Holy Shield uptime, Seal of Command, and add-control tools become more valuable.

🏃 Movement-heavy fight

  • Prioritize movement speed, hold ranged pickup tools, and accept imperfect Consecration uptime.

Icecrown Citadel: Lower Spire and Plagueworks

Lord Marrowgar

  • 10 & 25Saber Lash TanksStack tightly with the other tank for Saber Lash, face Marrowgar away from the raid, and restack immediately after Bone Storm.
  • 10-playerSharedBone Spike Graveyard impales one player per cast.
  • 25-playerSharedBone Spike Graveyard impales three players per cast.
  • 10 & 25NormalSaber Lash TanksBone Storm lasts about 20 seconds. Stay alive while moving, then re-establish the tank stack when it ends.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedBone Storm lasts about 30 seconds; Bone Spike Graveyard continues during it; and Coldflame persists for about eight seconds instead of three. Help free spikes when safe and keep the restack path clear.

Lady Deathwhisper

  • 10 & 25Add TankUse Consecration and Hammer of the Righteous for the wave, but hold Avenger's Shield for a caster, reanimated cultist, silence, or distant pickup.
  • 10-playerNormalSharedDominate Mind is not used.
  • 25-playerNormalSharedDominate Mind controls one player at a time; stop cleave and use assigned crowd control.
  • 10 & 25NormalThreat-swap TanksDeathwhisper is tauntable. Swap by taunt as Touch of Insignificance reduces the active tank's threat.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedCultist waves arrive about every 45 seconds instead of every 60 seconds.
  • 10-playerHeroicAdd TankPhase two continues with one cultist per wave, and one player can be mind-controlled.
  • 25-playerHeroicAdd TankPhase two continues with three cultists per wave, and three players can be mind-controlled. Vengeful Shade pressure is also higher.
  • 10 & 25HeroicThreat-swap TanksDeathwhisper is taunt-immune. The next tank must build and hold threat before the active tank is suppressed; do not rely on a taunt handoff.

Gunship Battle

  • 10 & 25Boarding TankJump first and call the jump so the boarding group does not arrive before its tank. Watch the opposing commander's stacking Battle Fury.
  • 10 & 25Boarding TankUse a cooldown or delay the next jump when Battle Fury remains high.
  • 10 & 25HeroicBoarding TankRocket Artillery can knock players back, and surviving enemy crew can reach Berserk. Leave enough time to exit safely.

Deathbringer Saurfang

  • 10 & 25Rune-of-Blood TanksSwap immediately at one stack of Rune of Blood.
  • 10-playerSharedEach Blood Beast wave contains two Beasts.
  • 25-playerSharedEach Blood Beast wave contains five Beasts.
  • 10 & 25SharedHellscream testedBlood Beasts are Beast-type enemies, so Holy Wrath does not stun them. This has also been confirmed directly in ICC 10 on Hellscream.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedScent of Blood makes the Beasts faster, the enrage timer drops from eight minutes to six, and a Marked player's death heals Saurfang for 20% instead of 5%. Protect Beast control and marked players.

Festergut

  • 10 & 25Gastric-Bloat TanksSwap at nine stacks of Gastric Bloat, before the tenth stack detonates.
  • 10 & 25Active TankPlan Divine Protection or an external cooldown for the high-Inhale tanking window.
  • 10-playerSharedGas Spore targets two players.
  • 25-playerSharedGas Spore targets three players.
  • 10 & 25HeroicActive TankProfessor Putricide throws Malleable Goo into the arena. Dodge it without turning Festergut or disrupting the tank handoff.

Rotface

  • 10 & 25Boss TankKeep Rotface centered and stable. Slime Spray targets a raid member; move out of the cone when he turns to channel it. There is no normal boss-tank swap.
  • 10 & 25Big Ooze TankHellscream testedEstablish threat on the Big Ooze with ranged attacks, then kite it around the room's edge. Small Oozes are taunt-immune on Hellscream.
  • 10 & 25Big Ooze TankKeep a clean route around flooded sections; Ooze Flood damages and slows anyone crossing it.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedVile Gas adds ranged disruption, and Mutated Infection applies a 75% healing reduction instead of 50%.
  • 10 & 25HeroicBig Ooze TankAvoid Vile Gas groups while preserving a clean path around the edge.

Professor Putricide

  • 10 & 25Boss TankKeep Putricide positioned for predictable Volatile Ooze and Gas Cloud movement.
  • 10 & 25Abomination DriverIn phases one and two, eat growing Slime Puddles and use Regurgitated Ooze to slow the fixated Volatile Ooze or Gas Cloud. They are not tanked.
  • 10 & 25Mutated-Plague TanksIn phase three, swap Mutated Plague on the raid's assigned stack count and layer cooldowns as damage ramps.
  • 10 & 25NormalSharedTear Gas controls the phase transitions, and only one experiment is active at a time.
  • 10-playerHeroicSharedThere is no Tear Gas transition. Unbound Plague must be passed, and a Volatile Ooze and Gas Cloud spawn together. Ooze Variable and Gas Variable are not used.
  • 25-playerHeroicSharedThere is no Tear Gas transition. Unbound Plague must be passed; a Volatile Ooze and Gas Cloud spawn together; and Ooze Variable or Gas Variable limits which target each player can damage.
  • 25-playerHeroicSharedMalleable Goo pressure is higher than on normal difficulty; keep movement lanes clear for the tank and melee group.
Icecrown Citadel: Crimson Hall and Frostwing

Blood Prince Council

  • 10 & 25Keleseth TankCollect Dark Nuclei with ranged attacks, avoid cleaving them down, and use a defensive while Shadow Resonance coverage is still low.
  • 10 & 25HeroicKeleseth TankShadow Prison punishes continuous movement. Move only as needed, then stop to clear the stacks.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedKinetic Bombs descend faster and demand tighter coverage. Do not abandon an assigned tank target to chase one without a call.

Blood-Queen Lana'thel

  • 10 & 25Tank PairStay together for Blood Mirror, keep Lana'thel positioned consistently, and use a defensive for dangerous paired-tank damage.
  • 10 & 25HeroicTank PairPresence of the Darkfallen increases Shroud of Sorrow damage by 5% per vampire. Coordinate raid defensives as the raid becomes bitten; this does not create a new tank swap.

Valithria Dreamwalker

  • 10 & 25Add TankFace Gluttonous Abominations away, control where they die, and cleanse Gut Spray promptly.
  • 10 & 25Add TankControl or kite Blistering Zombies and prioritize Blazing Skeletons. Holy Wrath works well on these Undead waves.
  • 10-playerSharedEach portal wave opens three Dream Portals.
  • 25-playerSharedEach portal wave opens eight Dream Portals.
  • 10 & 25HeroicAdd TankValithria steadily loses health; portals are not announced; Nightmare Clouds damage collectors and do not respawn; Risen Archmage Frostbolt Volley also burns mana; and Zombies move faster. Protect interrupts and kill priorities.

Sindragosa

  • 10 & 25SharedUse Frost Resistance Aura unless another aura is assigned.
  • 10 & 25Mystic-Buffet TanksPlan cooldowns for Frost Breath, swap to control Mystic Buffet, and react immediately to Blistering Cold.
  • 10-playerSharedEach air phase marks two players for Ice Tomb.
  • 25-playerNormalSharedEach air phase marks five players for Ice Tomb.
  • 25-playerHeroicSharedEach air phase marks six players for Ice Tomb.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedInstability's backlash damages nearby players. Anyone with Unchained Magic must isolate before casting; tanks should keep positioning predictable.
The Lich King

The Lich King

  • 10 & 25Add TankUse Holy Wrath to control Drudge Ghouls and Shambling Horrors. Cleanse Necrotic Plague only when assigned.
  • 10 & 25Transition TanksTaunt Raging Spirits promptly and keep their frontal cone away from the raid.
  • 10 & 25Boss TankPlan a personal or external cooldown for Soul Reaper.
  • 10-playerSharedEach Val'kyr Shadowguard carries one player.
  • 25-playerSharedEach Val'kyr wave contains three Shadowguards and can carry three players.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedShadow Traps are active in phase one. Keep boss and add movement deliberate so the raid has room to react.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedA Val'kyr releases its captive at 50% health, then remains active and can channel Life Siphon. Do not treat the drop as the end of the add.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedHarvest Souls affects the raid and sends it into Frostmourne, where Spirit Bombs and Wicked Spirits become the priority. Preserve cooldowns for the encounter plan.
Ruby Sanctum: Halion

Halion

  • 10 & 25Material-Realm TankFace Halion away, keep Flame Breath predictable, and maintain consistent positioning while the raid balances Corporeality.
  • 10 & 25Twilight-Realm TankFace Halion away, keep Dark Breath predictable, and prioritize Twilight Cutter positioning over perfect rotation uptime.
  • 10 & 25NormalSharedTwo Shadow Orbs create one rotating Twilight Cutter. Fiery Combustion and Soul Consumption zones remain in their own realms.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedFour Shadow Orbs create two perpendicular Twilight Cutters, and Combustion or Consumption zones affect both realms.
  • 10-playerHeroicSharedHeroic 10 does not add Living Inferno or Living Ember tanking; no separate Add Tank is required.
  • 25-playerHeroicAdd TankMeteor Strike spawns a Living Inferno and Living Embers. Separate the Inferno so Blazing Aura does not empower the Embers, and kill the Inferno first.
  • 10 & 25SharedReact to actual damage and healer movement rather than copying a fixed cooldown script from another server.
Trial of the Grand Crusader: Anub'arak

Anub'arak

  • 10-playerNormalAdd TankEach normal wave contains one Nerubian Burrower.
  • 25-playerNormalAdd TankEach normal wave contains two Nerubian Burrowers.
  • 10-playerHeroicAdd TankEach heroic wave contains two Nerubian Burrowers.
  • 25-playerHeroicAdd TanksEach heroic wave contains four Nerubian Burrowers, commonly split two and two between two Add Tanks.
  • 10 & 25HeroicAdd TankA block-focused set is useful. Hold Burrowers on Permafrost, prevent uncontrolled Spider Frenzy overlap, use Holy Wrath to stun these Undead adds, and interrupt Shadow Strike.
  • 10 & 25HeroicSharedOnly six Frost Spheres are available and they do not respawn. Burrowers continue during phase three.
  • 10 & 25NormalSharedLeeching Swarm drains 10% of each player's current health per tick.
  • 10-playerHeroicSharedLeeching Swarm drains 20% of each player's current health per tick.
  • 25-playerHeroicSharedLeeching Swarm drains 30% of each player's current health per tick.
  • 10-playerSharedPenetrating Cold targets two players.
  • 25-playerSharedPenetrating Cold targets five players.
