WotLK 3.3.5 Raiding with your Tankadin

Prepare your consumables, select the right set, and know which Paladin tools matter on each raid encounter.

Optional old-expansion physical absorbs
  • Greater Rune of Warding: temporary chest effect with a chance to absorb a small physical hit.

Encounter tuning

🛡️ Heavy physical damage

  • Favor armor, stamina, an armor trinket, and Indestructible Potions.

✨ Magic or mixed burst

  • Favor stamina, a resistance aura or on-use trinket, and planned external cooldowns.

🎯 Mandatory taunts or pickups

  • Use Glyph of Righteous Defense and add only enough hit for the required reliability.

⚔️ Parry-sensitive boss

  • Build extra expertise only after confirming that the boss parry-hastes on Hellscream.

🧱 Many smaller attackers

  • Block value, Holy Shield uptime, Seal of Command, and add-control tools become more valuable.

🏃 Movement-heavy fight

  • Prioritize movement speed, hold ranged pickup tools, and accept imperfect Consecration uptime.

Specific raid encounter notes

These are Tankadin responsibilities and useful Paladin tools, not full raid strategies. Follow the raid leader's positioning and assignment.

🧊 Icecrown Citadel: Lower Spire and Plagueworks

Lord Marrowgar

  • Stack with the other tank for Saber Lash and face him away from the raid.
  • Keep a ranged ability ready for Bone Spikes only when using it will not disturb boss control.
  • Use Divine Protection when Bone Storm ends if healers are still moving.

Lady Deathwhisper

  • Use Consecration and Hammer of the Righteous to establish add threat.
  • Hold Avenger's Shield for a caster, reanimated add, silence, or distant pickup.
  • In phase two, be ready for the assigned taunt swap.

Gunship Battle

  • If you jump, watch the opposing commander's stacking damage buff.
  • Use a cooldown or delay the next jump when stacks remain high.
  • Call the jump so healers and DPS do not arrive before the tank.

Deathbringer Saurfang

  • Swap on Rune of Blood as assigned.
  • Holy Wrath can stun Blood Beasts.
  • Peel loose beasts with ranged tools without turning or dragging the boss through the raid.

Festergut

  • Swap before Gastric Bloat becomes lethal.
  • Plan Divine Protection for high-Inhale periods.
  • Bubble-cancel a stack only when the co-tank is ready and the interaction has been tested on Hellscream.

Rotface

  • Boss tank: face Slime Spray away from the raid.
  • Ooze tank: use movement and ranged pickup tools.
  • Hellscream: Small Oozes are taunt-immune. Do not build the strategy around taunting them.

Professor Putricide

  • Keep the boss positioned for ooze and gas-cloud movement.
  • Save ranged snap threat when assigned to an add.
  • In phase three, swap on Mutated Plague and use planned cooldowns as damage ramps.
🩸 Icecrown Citadel: Crimson Hall and Frostwing

Blood Prince Council

  • If tanking Keleseth, collect Dark Nuclei with ranged tools.
  • Avoid cleaving or killing the nuclei that provide Shadow Resonance.
  • Use a defensive while nucleus coverage is still low.

Blood-Queen Lana'thel

  • The two tanks stay close enough for Blood Mirror.
  • Use a defensive for dangerous paired-tank damage windows.
  • Do not let movement mechanics separate the tank pair.

Valithria Dreamwalker

  • Prioritize Blazing Skeletons and dangerous Zombies.
  • Holy Wrath is excellent against the Undead add waves.
  • Face Abominations away and control their death position.

Sindragosa

  • Use Frost Resistance Aura unless the raid assigns another aura.
  • Plan cooldowns for Frost Breath and healer movement.
  • Swap to control Mystic Buffet and react immediately to Blistering Cold.
👑 The Lich King
  • Know whether you are the boss tank or the add tank before the pull.
  • Holy Wrath is valuable for Ghouls and Shambling Horrors.
  • Cleanse Necrotic Plague only when assigned; an early or late cleanse can ruin add control.
  • Taunt Raging Spirits promptly during transitions.
  • When boss tanking, plan a personal or external cooldown for Soul Reaper.
🔥 Ruby Sanctum: Halion
  • Face Halion away from the raid and keep the breath direction predictable.
  • Plan cooldowns around Flame or Dark Breath combined with movement.
  • Inside tank: cutter positioning outranks perfect rotation uptime.
  • Add tank: Seal of Command, Hammer, Consecration, and Hand of Reckoning are strong Living Ember tools.
  • React to actual damage and healer movement rather than using a fixed cooldown script from another server.
🪲 Trial of the Grand Crusader: Anub'arak
  • A block-focused set can be useful when tanking Nerubian Burrowers.
  • Holy Wrath stuns the Burrowers and helps stabilize add waves.
  • Interrupt Shadow Strike reliably when assigned; carry enough hit for the job.
  • Use Frost patches for controlled kiting and positioning.
  • Do not import solo-tank or overgeared private-server strategies as the default plan.
🧪 Hellscream checks before building a special set
  • Confirm taunt-hit behavior with combat logs.
  • Confirm whether the boss uses parry-haste.
  • Test Tier 10 bonuses, Divine Sacrifice cancellation, Nitro failures, and weapon proc enchants.
  • Change one variable at a time and keep the dependable general set ready.
