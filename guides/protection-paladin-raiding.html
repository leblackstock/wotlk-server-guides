Prepare your consumables, select the right set, and know which Paladin tools matter on each raid encounter.

Optional old-expansion physical absorbs
  • Greater Rune of Warding: temporary chest effect with a chance to absorb a small physical hit.
  • Greater Ward of Shielding: consumable shield ward that absorbs a fixed amount before breaking.
  • Verify that each works, stacks as expected, and is worth the inventory cost on Hellscream before stocking them.

Encounter tuning

🛡️ Heavy physical damage

  • Favor armor, stamina, an armor trinket, and Indestructible Potions.

✨ Magic or mixed burst

  • Favor stamina, a resistance aura or on-use trinket, and planned external cooldowns.

🎯 Mandatory taunts or pickups

  • Use Glyph of Righteous Defense and add only enough hit for the required reliability.

⚔️ Parry-sensitive boss

  • Build extra expertise only after confirming that the boss parry-hastes on Hellscream.

🧱 Many smaller attackers

  • Block value, Holy Shield uptime, Seal of Command, and add-control tools become more valuable.

🏃 Movement-heavy fight

  • Prioritize movement speed, hold ranged pickup tools, and accept imperfect Consecration uptime.

Icecrown Citadel: Lower Spire and Plagueworks

Lord Marrowgar

  • 10 & 25Both tanksStack with the other tank for Saber Lash and face him away from the raid.
  • 10 & 25SharedKeep a ranged ability ready for Bone Spikes only when using it will not disturb boss control.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksUse Divine Protection when Bone Storm ends if healers are still moving.

Lady Deathwhisper

  • 10 & 25Off-tankUse Consecration and Hammer of the Righteous to establish add threat.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankHold Avenger's Shield for a caster, reanimated add, silence, or distant pickup.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksIn phase two, be ready for the assigned taunt swap.

Gunship Battle

  • 10 & 25Assigned tankIf you jump, watch the opposing commander's stacking damage buff.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankUse a cooldown or delay the next jump when stacks remain high.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankCall the jump so healers and DPS do not arrive before the tank.

Deathbringer Saurfang

  • 10 & 25Both tanksTank swap at one stack of Rune of Blood.
  • 10-playerSharedHellscream testedHoly Wrath did not stun Blood Beasts in direct ICC 10 testing. Do not rely on it for Beast control.
  • 25-playerSharedTest on HellscreamHoly Wrath has not been confirmed against Blood Beasts in ICC 25. Do not build Beast control around it until tested.
  • 10 & 25SharedPeel loose beasts with ranged tools without turning or dragging the boss through the raid.

Festergut

  • 10 & 25Both tanksSwap before Gastric Bloat becomes lethal.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksPlan Divine Protection for high-Inhale periods.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksBubble-cancel a stack only when the co-tank is ready and the interaction has been tested on Hellscream.

Rotface

  • 10 & 25Main tankFace Slime Spray away from the raid.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankUse movement and ranged pickup tools while controlling the Big Ooze.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankHellscream testedSmall Oozes are taunt-immune on Hellscream. Do not build the strategy around taunting them.

Professor Putricide

  • 10 & 25Main tankKeep the boss positioned for ooze and gas-cloud movement.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankSave ranged snap threat when assigned to an add.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksIn phase three, swap on Mutated Plague and use planned cooldowns as damage ramps.
Icecrown Citadel: Crimson Hall and Frostwing

Blood Prince Council

  • 10 & 25Off-tankIf tanking Keleseth, collect Dark Nuclei with ranged tools.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankAvoid cleaving or killing the nuclei that provide Shadow Resonance.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankUse a defensive while nucleus coverage is still low.

Blood-Queen Lana'thel

  • 10 & 25Both tanksStay close enough to the other tank for Blood Mirror.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksUse a defensive for dangerous paired-tank damage windows.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksDo not let movement mechanics separate the tank pair.

Valithria Dreamwalker

  • 10 & 25Assigned tankPrioritize Blazing Skeletons and dangerous Zombies.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankHoly Wrath is excellent against the Undead add waves.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankFace Abominations away and control their death position.

Sindragosa

  • 10 & 25SharedUse Frost Resistance Aura unless the raid assigns another aura.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksPlan cooldowns for Frost Breath and healer movement.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksSwap to control Mystic Buffet and react immediately to Blistering Cold.
The Lich King
  • 10 & 25SharedKnow whether you are the boss tank or the add tank before the pull.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankHoly Wrath is valuable for Ghouls and Shambling Horrors.
  • 10 & 25Assigned tankCleanse Necrotic Plague only when assigned; an early or late cleanse can ruin add control.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksTaunt Raging Spirits promptly during transitions.
  • 10 & 25Main tankWhen boss tanking, plan a personal or external cooldown for Soul Reaper.
Ruby Sanctum: Halion
  • 10 & 25Both tanksFace Halion away from the raid and keep the breath direction predictable.
  • 10 & 25Both tanksPlan cooldowns around Flame or Dark Breath combined with movement.
  • 10 & 25Off-tank / insideCutter positioning outranks perfect rotation uptime.
  • 10 & 25Off-tank / addsSeal of Command, Hammer of the Righteous, Consecration, and Hand of Reckoning are strong Living Ember tools.
  • 10 & 25SharedReact to actual damage and healer movement rather than using a fixed cooldown script from another server.
Trial of the Grand Crusader: Anub'arak
  • 10 & 25Off-tankA block-focused set can be useful when tanking Nerubian Burrowers.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankHoly Wrath stuns the Burrowers and helps stabilize add waves.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankInterrupt Shadow Strike reliably when assigned; carry enough hit for the job.
  • 10 & 25Off-tankUse Frost patches for controlled kiting and positioning.
  • 10 & 25SharedDo not import solo-tank or overgeared private-server strategies as the default plan.
Hellscream checks before building a special set
  • Confirm taunt-hit behavior with combat logs.
  • Confirm whether the boss uses parry-haste.
  • Test Tier 10 bonuses, Divine Sacrifice cancellation, Nitro failures, and weapon proc enchants.
  • Change one variable at a time and keep the dependable general set ready.
