WotLK 3.3.5 Skinning + Leatherworking Materials AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers Skinning and Leatherworking materials, leathers, hides, scales, chitin, furs, conversion checks, vendor supplies, plus a few finished prices for major leg armors, armor kits, and drums.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Leather rule: Compare raw leather, processed leather, rare hides/scales, and finished leg armor value before converting or crafting. This server can have thin supply and weird bottlenecks.

Conversion / value checks

Use these as quick rules before converting leather or crafting processed materials.

ConversionRuleDemandNotes
Borean Leather → Heavy Borean Leather
Wrath conversion
 Use when Heavy Borean Leather sells above the value of the leather consumed. Very High Main Wrath LW conversion check. Do not convert automatically if raw Borean Leather is moving faster.
Heavy Borean Leather ↔ Arctic Fur value check
Dalaran pattern/trade relationship
 Compare 10 Heavy Borean Leather against 1 Arctic Fur before selling either item. Very High Heavy Borean Leather and Arctic Fur can anchor each other because pattern/trade systems use them.
Knothide Leather → Heavy Knothide Leather
TBC conversion
 Use only when Heavy Knothide premium beats raw Knothide value. Med-High Good for TBC levelers but slower than Wrath leather.
Scraps → Leather
Scrap cleanup
 Convert scraps when the finished leather sells better than raw scraps. Med Useful for bag cleanup and low-level mats; not usually a main gold maker.
Hide curing / older recipes
Classic/TBC odd mats
 Check the exact hide, salt/thread, and cured-hide demand before crafting. Low-Med Old cured hides are niche. Singles only.
