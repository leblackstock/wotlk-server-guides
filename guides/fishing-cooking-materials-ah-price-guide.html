WotLK 3.3.5 Fishing + Cooking Materials AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers raw fish, cooking meats, eggs, clams, pearls, rare catches, rare fishing poles/devices, vendor supplies, plus a few finished prices for major raid foods and feasts.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post small stacks. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Fishing/Cooking rule: Fish Feast fish, Northern Spices, Dragonfin Angelfish, and rare fishing gear can move very differently from ordinary leveling mats. Always check whether the item is actually tradeable on this server before pricing rare poles, hats, lines, or daily-bag rewards.
