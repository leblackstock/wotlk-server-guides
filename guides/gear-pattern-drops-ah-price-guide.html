WotLK 3.3.5 Gear Pattern Drops AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers specific sellable dropped Blacksmithing plans, Leatherworking patterns, and Tailoring patterns that unlock crafted gear or cosmetic gear. Consumable recipes, glyph books, enchants, schematics, and gem designs belong in separate recipe-drop guides.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Gear patterns sell as single items, not stacks. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Guide focus: AH-priceable gear-pattern drops only. Trainer, vendor, reputation, BoP, and non-gear recipes are outside this guide.
Tradeability note: Ulduar BS/LW/Tailoring recipes are included only where they appear unbound and tradeable. Known BoP patterns, such as Corehound Boots/Belt, are not AH-priceable here.
