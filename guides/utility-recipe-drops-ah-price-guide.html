WotLK 3.3.5 Utility Recipe Drops AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers specific AH-priceable Enchanting formulas, Jewelcrafting designs, and Engineering schematics that unlock useful services, gem cuts, enchants, scopes, reflectors, pets, or gadgets. Gear patterns, consumable recipes, glyph books, and BoP recipes belong outside this guide.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Utility recipes sell as single items, not stacks. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Guide focus: AH-priceable utility recipe drops only. BoP, trainer, vendor, reputation, token, and research-only recipes are outside this guide. Jewelcrafting entries are limited to WotLK world-drop BoE designs listed as tradeable.
Tradeability note: Useful BoP recipes such as Formula: Enchant Gloves - Angler and Schematic: Jeeves are not AH-priceable. Ulduar enchanting formulas should only be priced if this server makes them tradeable.
