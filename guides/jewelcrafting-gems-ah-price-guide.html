Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Sorted with epic gems, metas, Dragon's Eye, and Northrend materials first.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. Post singles for expensive gems. Use Quick for fast sales and High only when supply is scarce. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Cut-gem rule: Raw gems are the baseline. Popular cuts should sell for raw gem cost + cut premium. Do not cut every gem unless you know the market.

Strategy notes

Best movers

  • Cardinal Ruby top gem
    Usually the strongest raw/cut gem market.
  • Dragon's Eye token value
    Raw item can sell; cut JC gems are for jewelcrafters only.
  • Insightful / Austere / Relentless / Chaotic metas
    Popular meta cuts deserve a real cut premium.
  • Titanium Ore
    Prospecting value depends on epic gem and Dragon's Eye market.

Do not overcut

  • Do not cut every raw gem just because you can. Raw gems are more flexible.
  • Avoid flooding 8-day walls of the same cut gem.
  • For expensive gems, post singles first.
  • For cheap gems, small stacks help levelers and Icy Prism buyers.

Small reminders

Icy Prism check

Icy Prism uses Frozen Orb + Chalcedony + Shadow Crystal + Dark Jade. Compare material cost against the expected rare gems, epic gem chance, and Dragon's Eye chance.

Cut premium

For popular cuts, price from raw gem value plus a fair tip/premium. For unpopular cuts, raw gem value may be better than cutting.

Sources / calibration

Prices are low-pop starting points, not fixed market rules. They are calibrated from public profession references, Wrath-era gem demand, and practical reposting behavior. Always adjust for current AH supply, guild demand, and trade-chat pricing.
