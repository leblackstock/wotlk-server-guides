WotLK 3.3.5 Consumable & Misc Recipe Drops AH Price Guide

Low-pop AH pricing sheet for Hellscream / Garrosh. Covers specific AH-priceable Alchemy recipe drops, Cooking recipe drops, and the Book of Glyph Mastery. Gear patterns, Enchanting formulas, Jewelcrafting designs, Engineering schematics, vendor recipes, and BoP recipes belong outside this guide.

Read first: Use Target Buyout as the normal price. These recipes sell as single items, not stacks. AH durations are 4× normal: 12h ≈ 2 days, 48h ≈ 8 days.
Scope: This page is intentionally small. It catches the consumable/misc recipe drops that do not belong in the Gear Pattern Drops guide or the Utility Recipe Drops guide. BoP, trainer, vendor, reputation, and research-only recipes are not priced.
BoP audit: Major Protection Potion, Mighty Protection Potion, Haste Potion, Fel Mana Potion, and similar BoP alchemy drops are intentionally excluded from AH pricing.

Pricing rules

Post style

  • Post recipes/books as singles.
  • Use 12h/24h for most recipes; 48h only for Book of Glyph Mastery or very scarce classics.
  • Do not list multiple copies of the same slow recipe unless demand is proven.

Value logic

  • A famous finished consumable makes the recipe easier to sell.
  • Book of Glyph Mastery is priced higher because it unlocks random glyph recipes for scribes.
  • If a recipe is BoP on this server, remove it from this guide instead of pricing it.
