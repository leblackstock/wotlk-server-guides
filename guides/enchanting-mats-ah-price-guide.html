Strategy notes

Posting duration

  • Fast Wrath movers: 12h/2-day listings give better repost control.
  • Slow old-world mats: 24h–48h style listings are fine because deposits are low.
  • Rare/epic mats: long listings are okay when priced near Target or High.

Undercutting

  • If the current price is sane, undercut by 1 copper to 1 silver.
  • If the price is too high, list around this guide’s High price.
  • If the price is too low, wait, buy it out, or list small at Target. Do not join the crash.

Best movers

  • Infinite Dust volume
    Post 5s, 10s, and 20s. This is the bread-and-butter Wrath mat.
  • Greater Cosmic Essence premium
    Singles and 3-stacks sell well because recipes use awkward counts.
  • Dream Shard endgame
    Strong demand for good enchants. Do not dump.
  • Abyss Crystal high value
    Post singles first. Big-ticket enchants make these spike.

Low-pop traps

  • Old-world mats can be scarce
    That does not mean they sell fast. Leveling demand is real, but slow.
  • Do not flood 20-stack walls
    Post a few stacks. Relist after sales. Low-pop buyers hate bulk-only listings.
  • Essences beat dusts
    Greater Nether / Greater Eternal style mats often carry better profit than dust.

Stack setup

  • Dusts
    List 5, 10, and 20 stacks.
  • Essences
    List 1, 3, and 10 stacks.
  • Shards
    List mostly singles, then 2s/5s.
  • Crystals
    List singles. Only stack if multiple buyers are active.

Small legend / reminders

Colors

Item names use normal WoW quality colors. Target Buyout is gold because it is the main number to use; Target Bid is cyan.

When to use each price

Quick = fast sale. Target = normal listing. High = empty AH or scarce supply.
