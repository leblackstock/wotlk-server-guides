Guide Disclaimer

These guides are made by me as a regular player trying to help the server. They are not official, permanent, or guaranteed to be correct.

Prices, demand, supply, and the server economy can change quickly, especially on a low-pop realm. Treat these guides as starting points, not fixed rules. Please check the current Auction House before buying, selling, crafting, or reposting.

I make mistakes sometimes. Some numbers may be outdated, incomplete, or based on limited information, and whole items may be missing from a guide or list. Corrections, updates, and better market info are welcome.

Gameplay and class guides are practical references, not guarantees. Private-server scripting, encounter tuning, item behavior, and addons may differ from standard WotLK 3.3.5. Check current tooltips and test anything that behaves strangely.

I do not always know whether this server treats certain crafted, vendor, or special items as usable only by the maker or otherwise not resellable. If something listed cannot actually be sold, traded, or reposted, or if an item is missing, please let me know so I can update it.

These guides are not official server policy and are not endorsed by staff. I am not responsible for anyone losing gold from using them.

The goal is to help people make better AH decisions, not to tell anyone exactly what they must do.
